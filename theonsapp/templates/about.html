About this site,

Theon's List started out as a weekend assignment for week six of my cohort at The Iron Yard in Greenville. The assignment was to build a clone of Craigslist replicating the ability of the user to list items, upload images and allow sorting of listed items by price and date listed.

Later in the cohort I revisited this assignment to add API functionality using the Django REST Framework. Most recently, I picked this as one of my favorite sites that I have built and decided to expand on it with a much nicer interface and some fun styling using Materialize CSS. If you have any questions feel free to drop me a line. You can get in touch with me at jude.nowicki@gmail.com or visit my personal home page at judenowicki.com.
